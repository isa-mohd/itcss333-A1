Meet Your Kindergarten Teaching Team

Welcome to Sunshine Kindergarten! We are excited to introduce your child's dedicated teaching team, who are here to provide a nurturing and fun-filled learning experience.

Lead Teacher: Ahmed Yousif

With 12 years of experience in early childhood education, Ahmed Yousif brings a wealth of knowledge and creativity to the classroom, is passionate about fostering a love for learning through play, exploration, and hands-on activities.

Assistant Teacher: Ali Abdulla

Ali Abdulla is an enthusiastic and caring educator who has been working with young children for 6 years, he is committed to helping each child develop socially, emotionally, and academically.

Support Staff: Khaled and Ashraf

Our team is also supported by Khaled and Ashraf, who assist in providing personalized attention and care to ensure that each child has a wonderful experience at school. They are integral to our classroom, helping with everything from classroom management to creative projects.
